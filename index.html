Through the fog

-Chronic pain outreach for youth and young adults-

I am a young adult who has lived with chronic pain since childhood. I understand how challenging it can be to balance daily life, pursue long-term goals, and maintain hope while living with a chronic health condition. Through this project, I hope to create a place where youth and young adults living with chronic pain feel seen, supported, and understood.

Through the Fog is a place to learn, grow, and find hope. Here I share educational resources, practical tools, and personal experiences that have helped me better understand my own body and nervous system. My goal is not simply to help people manage pain, but to help them build meaningful, fulfilling lives alongside it. I hope this website helps reduce the stigma surrounding invisible illnesses, encourages open conversations about chronic pain, and connects people with resources and communities that remind them they are not alone. My hope is that every visitor leaves with a little more understanding, a little more confidence, and a little more hope than when they arrived. Chronic pain may be part of your story, but it does not have to define who you are. My hope is that this website helps you find your own path forward—one step at a time, through the fog.
